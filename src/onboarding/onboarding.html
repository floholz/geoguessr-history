GeoGuessr History

by @floholz

GeoGuessr History adds a better and more detailed game history to geoguessr.com

The extension adds a table to your activities page, where you can find all the key stats for all of your games in one just look. For even more details you can simply click one of the games from the table and be navigated to this game's summary page.
